Meet the Breaking Bad characters

Step into the morally complex universe of Breaking Bad as we explore the pivotal characters who shaped its destiny. Witness extraordinary transformations, intricate alliances, and the chilling consequences that unfold behind the rise and fall of an illicit empire.

Jesse Pinkman

Yo, what up, bitch? Jesse is Walter White's unpredictable partner, often struggling with the moral implications of their business.

Gustavo Fring

A calm and calculating drug lord. Gustavo Fring runs a vast criminal empire hidden behind Los Pollos Hermanos. His polite demeanor masks a chilling ruthlessness.

Saul Goodman

The ethically flexible criminal lawyer. When you're in a bind and need a solution, no matter how unconventional, Saul's your guy. Better call him!
